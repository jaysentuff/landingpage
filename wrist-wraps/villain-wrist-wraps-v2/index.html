How does the Belt Loop System work? +

Simply thread the wrap through the loop (just like a belt), pull to your desired tightness, and secure with velcro. It takes seconds and gives you the same tight support every time - no more fumbling with thumb loops.

Are these approved for competition? +

Yes! Our 16" Villain Wrist Wraps are approved by both USPA and IPL for all sanctioned competitions. You can train and compete with the same gear.

What's the difference between 16", 24", and 30" wraps? +

16" wraps are perfect for Olympic lifts, CrossFit, and movements requiring more wrist mobility. 24" and 30" provide maximum support for heavy powerlifting movements like bench press. Choose based on your primary training style.

Why no thumb loop? +

Thumb loops serve only one purpose - helping you put wraps on. During your actual lift, they do nothing for support. Our belt loop system eliminates this useless feature and gives you a better way to secure your wraps with consistent tension every time.

What material are they made from? +

Our wraps use a premium cotton/poly elastic blend that wicks moisture and maintains its elasticity over time. The stitching is reinforced to handle thousands of pounds without fraying.

How do I care for my wraps? +

Hand wash with warm water and light detergent, then air dry. Avoid machine washing, tumble drying, and ironing to maintain elasticity and extend the life of your wraps.
